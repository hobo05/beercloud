Bottle 0,33L. Hazy golden color with small, frothy, white head. Aroma is spicy (coriander) and vegetable. Taste is medium sweet and it follows aroma. Medium to full body, creamy texture, lively carbonation. This one looks kinda weird to me - like drinking Belgium witt on steroids. Leffe Blond and Brune were my first Belgium beers, many years ago and these are still their best beers for me. I've tried many beers brewed under name Leffe all these years, but nothing came close to these two.

nathanvc (953) - Brugge, BELGIUM - JAN 26, 2018
Pours clear golden with a small, off-white head. Typical 'Leffe' aroma of sugar, apple (sauce), artificial hops, white bread, (cookie) dough, something perfumed. It tastes medium sugary sweet and medium 'spicy' bitter with an immediate astringent effect from added hop aroma, leading to an alcoholic and almost wry finish. Medium body, slick/syrupy texture, soft carbonation. An Abbey Triple as it shouldn't be.
